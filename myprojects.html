PROJECTS

PERSONAL FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT APPLICATION

With three other SWE interns, I created this application used for budgeting and forecasting monthly spending. It enables users to assess their spending at the current point of the month, establish spending goals, and view a prediction of spending based off of past months data. I worked primarily on the frontend, which includes features such as a dynamic pie chart, line graphs, and dashboards.

WEATHER X FARMING PROJECT

With two other team members, I participated in a Hackathon where we were given 24 hours to complete a project. I integrated my knowledge of environmental science to lead the way in producing a program that scans in environmental data from a file in order to produce and interpret various line graphs which chart environmental variables overtime.

NONOGRAMS GAME

I developed a version of an interactive logic game called Nonograms, which utilizes exhaustive search in order to detect whether users have selected the proper squares of a grid in order to solve a puzzle. I used the model-view-controller design pattern to accomplish this.
